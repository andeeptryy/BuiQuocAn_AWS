### **Week 1 Achievements**

* Successfully set up a personal AWS account and secured initial credits, ready for hands-on practical labs.
* Grasped the fundamental shift from traditional On-premise infrastructure to Cloud computing, specifically the transition from CAPEX to OPEX.
* Understood the physical layout of the AWS Global Infrastructure (Data Centers, Availability Zones, Regions, Local Zones) to design highly available (HA) systems.
* Identified the various interfaces to manage AWS resources (Management Console, CLI, SDK) and understood the critical security distinction between Root and IAM users.
* Recognized the paradigm shift brought by Generative AI in software development, particularly the transition toward Autonomous Agents and Spec-Driven Development using AWS Kiro.
* Acquired essential Cost Optimization strategies, such as Right-sizing, leveraging Serverless models, and implementing AWS Budgets/Allocation Tags to prevent bill shocks.
* Mastered the 6 pillars of the AWS Well-Architected Framework, clearly differentiating between process-focused Operational Excellence and architecture-focused Reliability (e.g., self-healing, avoiding single points of failure).
